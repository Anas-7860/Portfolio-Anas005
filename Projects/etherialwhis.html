Anonymous Dream & Secret Sharing Platform Built With Prompt Engineering

Ethereal Whispers is a unique and mysterious platform that provides a safe space for people to anonymously share their deepest dreams and darkest secrets. Built entirely through prompt engineering techniques, this project was casually created for fun and exploration of AI-driven development. The platform offers users complete anonymity while sharing their innermost thoughts, dreams, aspirations, and secrets without fear of judgment. With a dark, ethereal aesthetic and intuitive interface, users can browse through whispers from others, finding solace in shared experiences or simply releasing their own burdens into the digital void. It's a therapeutic digital sanctuary where vulnerability meets technology.
